About me

Hello! I’m Tracy, a senior product designer at Daybreak. I’ve been there for almost 4 years now. In 2022 I graduated from SFU SIAT design program.

At Daybreak.Studio

Currently, I lead a small team of 2 very talented product designers. Mentoring people and seeing them grow is one of the most fulfilling parts of my role.

On a craft-specific note, I’m a fan of creating a novel experiences and building on ambitious ideas. I think that's what gives people the "woah cool!" moment when using a product.
Leisurely passions

Outside of work I spend my time thinking of what to paint next. I typically spend around 3 days per painting but now I want to take my time and create something truly special to my heart.

I’m always attending local art and makers events happening around me, I love meeting cool designers and artists. Fruit shopping in chinatown and taking walks by the lakeshore also brings me joy.
